## Which Style to Use

Confirm with your faculty advisor in Fall 2026. Most education and CS education programs use **APA 7th edition**. ACM publications use their own style for submissions.

| Context | Style |
|---|---|
| Dissertation | APA 7th edition (confirm with program) |
| SIGCSE / ACM submissions | ACM Reference Format |
| IEEE submissions | IEEE citation style |
| Annotated bibliography | APA 7th edition |
| Reading notes | APA 7th edition |

## APA 7th Edition — Quick Reference

### Journal Article
```
Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of article. Title of Journal, volume(issue), page–page. https://doi.org/xxxxx
```
**Example:**
> Guzdial, M. (2003). A media computation course for non-majors. *Proceedings of ACM SIGCSE*, *35*(1), 104–108. https://doi.org/10.1145/792548.792586

### Book
```
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of work: Capital letter also for subtitle (Edition if not first). Publisher.
```
**Example:**
> Margolis, J., & Fisher, A. (2002). *Unlocking the clubhouse: Women in computing*. MIT Press.

### Edited Book Chapter
```
Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of chapter. In E. E. Editor (Ed.), Title of book (pp. xxx–xxx). Publisher.
```

### Report / Government Document
```
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of report (Report No. xxx). Publisher. URL
```
**Example:**
> Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration. (2014). *SAMHSA's concept of trauma and guidance for a trauma-informed approach* (HHS Publication No. SMA 14-4884). SAMHSA. https://store.samhsa.gov/product/SAMHSA-s-Concept-of-Trauma-and-Guidance-for-a-Trauma-Informed-Approach/SMA14-4884

### Conference Proceedings (ACM)
```
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of paper. In Proceedings of the [Conference Name] (pp. xxx–xxx). ACM. https://doi.org/xxxxx
```

### Dissertation
```
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of dissertation [Doctoral dissertation, Institution Name]. Repository Name. URL
```

## ACM Reference Format — Quick Reference

Used for SIGCSE, ICER, TOCE submissions.

```
[1] Author Last, First, and Author Last, First. Year. Title of paper. In Proceedings of Conference Name (Location, Month Day–Day, Year) (ABBREV 'YY). ACM, New York, NY, USA, Pages. https://doi.org/xxxxx
```

## Zotero Tips

- **Always import via DOI or URL** rather than typing manually — eliminates errors
- **Check every auto-imported record** — Zotero frequently misses volume/issue or gets author order wrong
- **Use the "Extra" field** for notes that don't fit standard fields
- **Create collections** that mirror your literature review themes
- **Use group libraries** if you want to share your library with your advisor
- Install the **Zotero browser connector** to save directly from ACM DL, Google Scholar, and IEEE Xplore
- Export to **APA 7th** for your annotated bibliography and dissertation

## Common APA 7 Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | Correct APA 7 Practice |
|---|---|
| Italicizing article titles | Only *journal names* and *book titles* are italicized |
| Including city of publication for books | Not required in APA 7 |
| Using "Retrieved from" before a URL | Drop "Retrieved from" unless access date matters |
| p. for page ranges in journals | Use page–page without "p." for journals |
| Abbreviating journal names | Spell out full journal name |
| Missing DOI | Always include DOI if available |
