## Why this course

AI literacy is Dissertation Topic D. Being able to engage rigorously with deep learning at a technical level — not just conceptually — is what enables research into how AI literacy should be framed for Christian higher education contexts. This course is the technical grounding for that argument.

---

## Connection to Dissertation Topic D

Dissertation Topic D: *AI literacy frameworks for Christian higher education* — deep learning fundamentals provide the technical baseline for arguing what an AI-literate undergraduate curriculum at a faith-based institution should include.
