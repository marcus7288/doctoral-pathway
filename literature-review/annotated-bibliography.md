## How to Use This File

- Add entries **only after completing an analytical read** — not when you save an article to Instapaper
- One entry per source: full APA citation + 3–5 sentence annotation
- Annotation should address: (1) main argument, (2) methodology/type of work, (3) relevance to your research
- Organize entries under the theme that best fits your literature review
- Flag entries you plan to cite in the dissertation with `→ CITE`
